### How to Donate

1. **Find a Project:** Browse or search for projects
2. **Click "Donate":** On the project card or detail page

## Understanding Quadratic Funding

### What is Quadratic Funding?

QF is a democratic way to fund public goods that:
- Values the **number of contributors** over donation size
- Matches small donations more generously
- Prevents wealthy donors from dominating
- Encourages community participation

### How It Works

1. **Donation Phase:** People donate to projects
2. **Matching Calculation:** QF formula calculates matches
3. **Distribution:** Matching funds distributed from pool
4. **Results:** Projects with broad support get more matching
